WILDLIFE info

Alligators

Alligators were hunted at one time almost to the point of extinction. Because of this alligators are protected by both state and federal regulations. It is illegal to hunt or trap a wild alligator without a permit.

Dolphins

Our seas are blessed with many magnificent creatures. Among them are the beautiful and graceful dolphin. Known for their intelligence and their docile nature they are truly a treasure.

Manatees

Manatees are such wonderful creatures. A gentle giant of sorts they are endangered, by federal guidelines, partly do to human contact or encroachment, boating accidents, litter and more.
